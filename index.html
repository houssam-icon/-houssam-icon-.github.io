About Me

Hello! I’m Hossam, a 19-year-old anime analyst from Morocco. With a passion for exploring deep themes in anime, I bring a unique perspective to my content. Currently studying at FST Tanger and having completed the ALX AI Career Essentials (AiCE) program, I combine my academic insights with my enthusiasm for anime to delve into its complex themes and characters.

My analysis focuses on uncovering the societal reflections and philosophical questions embedded in series like Attack on Titan and Naruto. My content, created for platforms like TikTok and YouTube Shorts, aims to engage and inspire fellow anime fans by offering fresh perspectives and sparking meaningful conversations.

My Content

I specialize in analyzing anime themes, focusing on series such as Attack on Titan and Naruto. My content uncovers the deeper meanings, societal reflections, and character motivations behind iconic anime series.

I’ve produced several engaging shorts, including themes like "The Walls in AOT: A Prison or Protection?" and "Freedom vs. Confinement in Attack on Titan". I am committed to expanding my analysis to more anime topics and series.
